Cookie Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This site uses technical cookies to ensure the correct functioning of its pages. We do not use profiling or tracking cookies.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites save on your device while browsing. They help remember information about your visit (such as language preference) and improve the browsing experience.

2. Cookies Used

  • Essential technical cookies: required for the site to function (session management, language preferences). No consent required.
  • Third-party cookies: Google Fonts and Google Maps may install their own technical cookies. For details, see their respective policies.

3. How to Disable Cookies

You can manage cookie preferences directly from your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Disabling technical cookies may affect some site functionality.

4. Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy. Changes will be published on this page.
