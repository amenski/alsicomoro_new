About us

A table that bridges two shores.

Al Sicomoro was born from the meeting of two cultures and one shared idea of hospitality: the warmth of a shared table, the slow rhythm of spices, the comfort of injera arriving at the centre like a map of flavours.

Every morning we prepare injera by tradition, letting the teff dough ferment for days. The meats are marinated in our house berbere — a blend of over twelve spices toasted and ground by us.

"Eating with your hands is not folklore. It is a way of remembering that food passes through the heart first." — Al Sicomoro family

The table experience

Four things that make us different.

— 01

Homemade injera

Three days of natural teff fermentation. No industrial flour, no shortcuts. Every morning we start from scratch.

— 02

House berbere

Twelve spices toasted and ground by us. It is the signature of every dish and cannot be found in any other kitchen in Milan.

— 03

Always shared.

One large plate in the centre of the table, eaten with hands. Cooking is gesture, story, memory. Never just food.

— 04

Coffee ceremony

After the meal, on request, the Ethiopian coffee ceremony. Beans roasted at the table, incense, three cups. Forty minutes of shared silence.
